Instagram Privacy Policy

Senury

Effective Date: March 29, 2025

1. Introduction

Senury ("we," "us," "our") values your privacy. This Instagram Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the Senury app ("the App") with your Instagram account.

2. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: When you connect your Instagram account, we collect basic profile information necessary for authentication and to enable the App's functionality.
  • Access Tokens: We securely store Instagram access tokens required for posting content on your behalf.
  • Usage Data: We collect non-personally identifiable information to enhance the user experience, such as usage statistics and error logs.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • We use the information collected solely for providing the functionality of the App, including:
    • Authenticating your Instagram account
    • Facilitating the upload and post process on Instagram
    • Improving the App's features and performance

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data. All Instagram access tokens are encrypted and stored securely. We do not store your Instagram login credentials.

5. Instagram API Usage

The App uses the official Instagram Graph API to facilitate content uploads. By using the App, you agree to Instagram's privacy policy and terms of service. We only access the minimum permissions required to post content on your behalf.

6. Data Retention

We retain Instagram access tokens only for as long as necessary to provide the service. You can revoke our access to your Instagram account at any time through your Instagram settings. When you revoke access, we will delete all associated tokens.

7. Your Rights

  • You can revoke our access to your Instagram account at any time
  • You can request deletion of your data associated with our App
  • You can view and manage the permissions granted to our App through your Instagram settings

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Instagram Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Effective Date" at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Instagram Privacy Policy, please contact us at contact@senury.com.
