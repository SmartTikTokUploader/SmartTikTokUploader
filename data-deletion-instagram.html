Instagram Data Deletion Instructions

Senury

Last Updated: March 29, 2025

This guide explains how to delete your Instagram-related data from Senury. You have the right to request deletion of your personal data at any time.

Method 1: Automatic Deletion through Instagram

  1. Log in to your Instagram account
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Select "Security"
  4. Choose "Apps and Websites"
  5. Find "Senury" in the list of connected apps
  6. Click "Remove" or "Revoke Access"

This will automatically revoke our access to your Instagram account and trigger the deletion of your data from our systems.

Method 2: Manual Deletion Request

If you prefer to request manual deletion of your data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to privacy@senury.com with the subject line "Instagram Data Deletion Request"
  2. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your Instagram username
    • The email address associated with your Senury account
    • A statement confirming your request for data deletion

What Data Will Be Deleted

When you request data deletion, we will remove:

  • Your Instagram access tokens
  • Any stored Instagram profile information
  • Usage data associated with your Instagram account
  • Any cached content or metadata

Important Notes

  • Data deletion requests are typically processed within 30 days
  • We may retain certain information if required by law or for legitimate business purposes
  • Deleting your data will prevent you from using Instagram features in the Senury app until you reconnect your account
  • You can always reconnect your Instagram account after deletion if you wish to use our services again

Contact Us

If you have any questions about data deletion or need assistance, please contact our privacy team at privacy@senury.com.
